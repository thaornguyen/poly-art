Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Poly Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Poly Puzzle is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require an account and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information. The app may process or store the following data:

  • Photos you choose to upload. When you create a custom puzzle, the selected image is processed entirely on your device (background removal and polygon generation). The image and generated puzzle are saved only in the app's private storage on your device. They are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Game progress and preferences. Filled pieces, unlocked artworks, hint counts, selected language, and similar settings are stored locally using the operating system's shared preferences.
  • Advertising identifiers. We display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect an advertising ID and limited device information to serve and measure ads. You can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • Crash and diagnostic data. The underlying platform (Google Play Services / Apple) may collect anonymous crash reports to help us improve stability.

2. On-Device Processing

Custom puzzle generation uses Google ML Kit Subject Segmentation and image-processing algorithms that run on your device. Your photos are not transmitted to us or any third party as part of this process.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide core gameplay: saving progress, unlocking content, running hints.
  • To display relevant advertising through AdMob.
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob — serves rewarded video ads. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google ML Kit — on-device subject segmentation. ML Kit Terms.

5. Children's Privacy

Poly Puzzle is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising content delivered through AdMob is configured to comply with applicable family policies; however, we recommend parental supervision for younger players.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. To delete it, you can clear the app's storage from your device settings or uninstall the app. Deleting a custom puzzle inside the app removes the corresponding files from the app's private storage.

7. Your Choices

  • Disable personalized ads via your device's advertising settings.
  • Revoke photo/camera permissions at any time in system settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.
